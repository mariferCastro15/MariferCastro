  • 1 cup heavy cream (whipping cream)

‚úøInstructions‚úø

  1. Lightly grease a 9x9-inch square baking dish or a similar-sized pan with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs with melted butter until the crumbs are evenly coated.
  3. Press the crumb mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan to form a crust.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, freshly squeezed lemon juice, lemon zest, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until well combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. You can use a hand mixer or a stand mixer on medium-high speed for this.
  6. Gently fold the whipped cream into the lemon mixture until fully incorporated. Take your time to ensure a smooth and airy texture.
  7. Pour the lemon and cream mixture over the graham cracker crust in the pan. Use a spatula to spread the mixture evenly.
  8. Cover the pan with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and place it in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. Garnish with additional lemon zest, if desired, for an extra burst of citrus aroma and flavor.
